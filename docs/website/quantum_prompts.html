What Are Quantum Prompts?

Quantum Prompts are meticulously crafted instructions designed to elicit nuanced, multi-faceted responses from Large Language Models (LLMs). They go beyond simple questions, incorporating context, constraints, persona, and desired output formats to guide the AI towards more precise, creative, or analytical results, mirroring the complex nature of EGOS itself.
